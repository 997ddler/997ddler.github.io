Wenhao Zhao

I am a PhD student at the College of Design and Engineering, National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. Dianbo Liu. I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science from Beihang University in 2024.

My research focuses on vector quantization, discrete representation learning, and multimodal generative models, with recent work on improving tokenization strategies for both vision and biological sequence modeling.
