Wenhao Zhao

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the College of Design and Engineering, National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Dianbo Liu. Prior to this, I obtained my B.Eng. in Computer Science from Beihang University in 2024.

My research lies at the intersection of vector quantization, discrete representation learning, and multimodal generative models. I focus on developing principled tokenization strategies that bridge continuous and discrete representations, with applications spanning computer vision and biological sequence modeling.
